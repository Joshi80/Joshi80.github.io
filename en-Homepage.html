Hello, I'm Joshua and I warmly welcome you to this website.

Here you will find what I do

Planes

Planes have been a passion of mine for a long time, and with them came aviation photography into my life, which combines two hobbies.

Aerial Photography

Aerial photos, taken with a drone, offer a unique perspective on the world from above. This type of photography allows us to see landscapes, buildings, cities, and other environments from a completely new angle.

Snapshots

Often, the best pictures are taken spontaneously and without much preparation, as the perfect moment for a photo can rarely be paused.

Long Exposure

Long exposure offers the possibility to depict motion and life in a single photo.

Astrophotography

Recently, I have also been trying my hand at Astrophotography. Due to many interfering factors such as weather, moon, and dew, this type of photography is accompanied by a slow learning process.

Events

Event photography captures the unforgettable moments and the unique atmosphere of an event in vibrant, expressive images.
